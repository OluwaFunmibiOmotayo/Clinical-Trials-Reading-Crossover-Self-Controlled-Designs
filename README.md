# Reading Assignment on Crossover and Self-Controlled Designs in Clinincal Trials by Louis T.A., Lavori P.W., Bailar J.C., and Polansky M.
## Assignment Overview
This assignment reviews the paper on crossover and self-controlled study designs in clinical research. The reading covers the following concepts::
- Parallel and crossover design
- Power of the Crossover Design over the parallel design
- Efficiency and Limitations of the crossover design
- Factors to consider before designing a crossover study
- Comparing Parallel design adjusted for sex with a crossover design
- Other study not considered as a crossover design
- Critical issues in self-controlled studies
